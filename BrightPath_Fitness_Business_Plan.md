## 1. Executive Summary

BrightPath Fitness is a subscription-based personal fitness tracking platform built for individuals who want a structured, private, and intelligent way to manage their training. The platform combines AI-powered workout logging, weight and BMI tracking, custom program design, and progress photo journaling in a single cohesive web application installable on any mobile device without an app store.

The business is currently in closed beta with a working product, zero technical debt, a verified email delivery pipeline, and a live deployment at fitness.brightpathtechnology.io. The platform is built on modern, low-cost infrastructure that scales linearly with revenue.

## 3. Problem & Opportunity

### The Problem

Fitness enthusiasts face a fragmented tracking experience:

- **Generic apps** (MyFitnessPal, Strava) are built for mass markets and lack program customization
- **Personal trainer software** (Trainerize, TrueCoach) targets trainers, not independent users
- **Spreadsheets and notebooks** are the default fallback — tedious and disconnected
- **Privacy concerns** are real: mainstream fitness apps monetize user data through advertising partnerships

The result is that motivated individuals either over-invest in complex systems or abandon tracking entirely within weeks.

### The Opportunity

The Canadian fitness app market is estimated at $480M CAD in 2026, growing at 12% annually (CAGR). The subset of users seeking privacy-first, customizable tracking with AI assistance is underserved. BrightPath Fitness targets this niche directly.

**Target user profile:**
- Age 28–55
- Gym-attending 2–5 days per week
- Works with a personal trainer or follows a self-directed program
- Values data privacy
- Willing to pay for a premium, ad-free experience
- Based in Ontario, Canada (primary); English Canada (secondary)

## 4. Product Description

BrightPath Fitness is a progressive web application (PWA) that functions as a native app on iPhone and Android without requiring App Store distribution.

### Core Features (Live)

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **AI Workout Parser** | Log a full session by typing a description or uploading a photo of handwritten notes. AI extracts exercises, sets, reps, and weight automatically. |
| **Weight & BMI Tracking** | Daily weigh-ins with trend charts, BMI calculation, body fat percentage, and target tracking. |
| **Custom Program Design** | User-defined weekly session targets, calorie goals, preferred training locations, and short/long-term goals. |
| **Progress Photos** | Before/after photo journaling with date and caption, stored privately and never shared. |
| **Session History** | Full exercise log with per-session calorie estimates, searchable history, and weekly summaries. |
| **Email Reminders** | Opt-in nudges when workout or weigh-in logs fall behind (3-day workout / 7-day weight thresholds). |
| **Mobile Installation** | Installable as a home screen app on iOS (Safari) and Android (Chrome) — no app store required. |
| **Invite-only Access** | Closed beta with admin-controlled invite codes; invite emails sent directly from within the app. |

### Differentiators

1. **AI-first logging** — no manual exercise entry required
2. **Privacy by design** — PIPEDA-compliant, no ads, no third-party data sharing, Canadian-hosted data
3. **Trainer-linkable** — designed to complement, not replace, personal training relationships
4. **Owned infrastructure** — no dependency on third-party fitness ecosystems

## 7. Business Model & Pricing

### Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic workout logging (manual), weight tracking, 30-day history |
| **BrightPath Premium** | $9.99/month or $79.99/year | All features: AI logging, unlimited history, progress photos, reminders, custom program |
| **BrightPath Coach** | $19.99/month | Premium + trainer dashboard view, client progress sharing, branded reports |

**Launch strategy:** Close beta period → open free tier → convert with Premium trial → introduce Coach tier in Year 2.

### Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription revenue** — primary, recurring, predictable
2. **Annual plan discount** — improves cash flow and reduces churn (Year 1: $79.99 vs $119.88 annual monthly)
3. **Trainer partnership program** — gyms and personal trainers resell Premium subscriptions at wholesale ($6.99/seat) with a BrightPath Trainer portal (Year 2)
4. **White-label licensing** — branded deployments for gyms and corporate wellness programs (Year 3)

## 8. Marketing & Growth Strategy

### Phase 1 — Referral-Led Growth (Months 1–6)

The current invite-only model is a deliberate referral engine. Each user can invite friends directly from the app. Goals:

- Grow to 50 beta users through personal training community
- Gather testimonials and usage data
- Refine onboarding and AI parsing quality

**Cost: $0** (product-led growth)

### Phase 2 — Community & Trainer Partnerships (Months 6–12)

- Partner with 5–10 independent personal trainers in Ontario (McDonald YMCA and similar facilities)
- Trainers recommend BrightPath to clients; receive 20% revenue share on referrals
- Launch Instagram and TikTok presence with short-form workout logging demos
- Google Ads targeting "fitness tracking app Canada" and "personal training log app"

**Budget: $500–$1,000/month**

### Phase 3 — Paid Acquisition & App Store (Year 2)

- Launch native iOS/Android apps via Capacitor
- Apple App Store and Google Play listing
- App Store Optimization (ASO)
- Increase Google Ads budget; test Meta Ads

**Budget: $2,000–$3,000/month**

### Projected Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

| Channel | Est. CAC |
|---|---|
| Referral (trainer) | $0–$15 |
| Social media organic | $5–$20 |
| Google Ads | $25–$60 |
| App Store organic | $10–$30 |

**Blended CAC target: <$35 CAD**

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

At $9.99/month with average churn of 4%/month:  
**LTV = $9.99 / 0.04 = $250 CAD**

## 12. Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly fixed costs (Year 1):** ~$510 CAD  
**Revenue per subscriber:** $9.99 CAD  
**Variable cost per subscriber:** ~$0.50 (AI API + email)  
**Contribution margin per subscriber:** $9.49

**Break-even = $510 / $9.49 = 54 subscribers**

At the projected growth rate, break-even is reached in **August 2026** (Month 5 of paid operations).
